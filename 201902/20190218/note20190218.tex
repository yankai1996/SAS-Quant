We do one-way-sort using $z$-score:
$$
\begin{aligned}
& z_{RD} = mean(z_1, z_2, z_3) \\
& z_i = z(RD_i/MC) \\
& RD_1 = RD_t \\
& RD_2 = (RD_t + 0.8RD_{t-1} + 0.6RD_{t-2} + 0.4RD_{t-3} + 0.2RD_{t-4})/3 \\
& RD_3 = (RD_t + 0.8RD_{t-1} + 0.6RD_{t-2})/2.4 
& \\
& z_{EMP} = z(|\frac{\Delta EMP}{COG}|) \\
& \Delta EMP = EMP_t - EMP_{t-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

Finally, we have
$$
z = z_{RD} + z_{EMP}
$$
